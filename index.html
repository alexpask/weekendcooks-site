Tomato Roti Jala

Roti jala (“net bread”) is a lacy pancake traditionally served during Eid (Hari Raya) in Malaysia. Traditionally turmeric is added to turn the batter a distinctive yellow colour. But today we decided to put our own twist on it – we created tomato roti jala.

Best served with curries containing lots of gravy.
